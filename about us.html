About Us

Discover our purpose, vision, and the people behind the movement.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate youth, HeyYouth started as a local initiative to teach underprivileged children in South Tangerang. Since then, we have grown into a youth-led education movement with volunteers, mentors, and diaspora collaborators across Indonesia and beyond. Even through the pandemic, we adapted by moving our learning and seminars online, ensuring continued impact.

Our Vision

To become a global youth organization that drives equal access to quality education and empowers the young generation for Indonesia's Golden Era in 2045.

Our Mission

  • 📘 Providing weekend classes in Math, Science, and English
  • 🌐 Hosting international seminars with experts and diaspora
  • 📚 Creating a book club to foster critical thinking and awareness
  • 🎁 Running charity programs to support children in need
