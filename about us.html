About HeyYouth

HeyYouth is a youth-driven community dedicated to transforming education across Indonesia. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education and mentorship opportunities.

Our mission is to connect passionate young leaders with communities that need educational support, creating a sustainable ecosystem of learning and growth that will help shape Indonesia's golden future by 2045.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've made significant strides in educational empowerment. Our programs have reached over 5,000 children across Indonesia, providing them with quality education, mentorship, and life skills.

We've built a network of 1,200+ dedicated volunteers and 500+ experienced mentors who work tirelessly to make education accessible to all, regardless of background or circumstances.

Vision

To create an Indonesia where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential by 2045.

Mission

Empowering communities through education by connecting youth mentors with children in need, fostering growth and creating lasting impact.
